## Game Rules
- The game is played on a 3*3 Grid.
- The human player uses X.
- The AI player uses 0.
- Players takes turns placing their marks.
- The first players align three marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the game.
- If all faces are filled and no players wins, the games ends in a Draw.
